Biography

Dr. Anni is a visionary leader and a pioneering cardiac surgeon with over 30 years of experience in healthcare delivery and hospital administration. Her journey began at prestigious medical institutions, where she excelled in complex cardiac procedures before transitioning into senior leadership roles.

As the Director of AnniCares Hospital, Dr. Anni has been instrumental in modernizing our healthcare infrastructure and implementing patient-centric protocols that meet international standards. Her leadership is defined by a commitment to medical ethics, innovation, and compassionate care.
